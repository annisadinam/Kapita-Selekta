Timeline Implementasi Bisnis

Fase 1: Foundation (6 Bulan)

Pembentukan legal entity, development portfolio, building network, dan acquiring first clients. Fokus pada project kecil dan medium untuk membangun reputasi.

Fase 2: Growth (12 Bulan)

Expansion team, developing proprietary tools, scaling operations, dan targeting larger projects. Implementasi sistem dan proses yang scalable.

Fase 3: Scale (18 Bulan)

Market expansion, product development, partnership building, dan exploring funding opportunities. Fokus pada innovation dan market leadership.

Fase 4: Maturity (24+ Bulan)

Establishment as industry leader, continuous innovation, international expansion, dan strategic acquisitions. Focus on sustainable growth dan impact.
